About me

My name is Nick and I'm a content creator from Ottawa, Canada.

I picked up my dad's vhs camcorder at the start of 2020 and started filming everything I could. I fell in love with analog content ever since.

Naturally, I started taking 35mm photos - mainly portraits of my friends and events I would attend.

Ever since, I've established a network with other creatives in the national capital region and offer a wide variety of services including 35mm photos, super 8 videos, vhs and other analog content.

Feel free to reach out if you would like to book me or have any questions about my services.
